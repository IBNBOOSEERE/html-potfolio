Angela Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For dough:

  • ¼ cup warm milk
  • 2¼ teaspoon yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ½ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1½ tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast,sugar and eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combine into a dough.
  4. knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.
  6. Leave it somewhere to raise for 2 hours.
  7. After the dough has double in size, roll it out into a large rectangle.
  8. Melt the butter for filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  9. Spread the filling onto the dough and then roll the dough into a Swiss roll.
  10. Cut the roll into a 3cm section and place the flat into a baking tray.
  11. Pre-heat the oven to 350F or 180C, then beak the rolls for 20-25min until lightly brown.
